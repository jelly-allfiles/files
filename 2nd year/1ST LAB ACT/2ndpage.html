Importance of Conservation of Our Sea and Ocean.

  • Maintains biodiversity and ecosystem balance
  • Supports livelihoods and food security for millions of people
  • Regulates climate and weather patterns
  • Provides oxygen through marine plant photosynthesis
  • Offers recreational and tourism opportunities.
  • Sustains coastal communities and economies
  • Acts as a carbon sink, helping mitigate climate change
  • Preserves cultural and historical heritage
  • Supports research, education, and innovation efforts
