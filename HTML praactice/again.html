Sweet and Sour Pork

This Sweet and Sour Pork Recipe is all that you need to make a good Sweet and Sour Pork dish. It is simple, straightforward, and easy to follow.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork sliced
  • 1 piece red bell pepper
  • 1 piece green bell pepper
  • 3 toublespoons soy sauce
  • 1 piece red onion wedged
  • 1 piece carrot sliced
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 1/4 cup water
  • 3 1/2 tablespoon vinegar
  • 5 tablespoons tomato ketchup
  • 4 tablespoons white sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Place the pork loin in a bowl. Add sou sauce. Marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. Pour the cooking oil in a pan then apply heat.
  3. Combine the flour and cornstarch in a bowl then mix until both ingredients are evenly distributred.
  4. Dip the marinated pork in beaten egg then on the flour and cornstarch mixture.
  5. Deep fry the pork for 10 minutes or until the color turns medium brown. Set aside.
  6. Heat a separate pan then pour-in the sweet and sour sauce ingredients.
  7. Add the carrots. Cook for 3 minutes.
  8. Put-in the onions and bell pepper. Cook for 4 minutes.
  9. Add the deep-fried pork loin and stir. Cook for 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  10. Transfer to a serving dish and serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
