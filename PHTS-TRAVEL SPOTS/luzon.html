Philippine Tourist Spot

"Traveling is the best way to feed the soul and explore the world."




       







Luzon:



Whale Shark


In Donsol, Sorsogon in the Bicol Region, you can swim with whale sharks.
If you love the sea and the majestic creatures that inhabit it, then Donsol whale
shark tours are a must-try for you. One of l give you the chance to observe whale
sharks in their natural habitat.


Binondo Chinatown

Barely an hour away from the Ninoy Aquino International Airport (NAIA)or
Manila Airport sits the oldest Chinatown in the world. Discover one of the best
Manila tourist spots, Binondo, situated right in the heart of the country's city capital.


Rizal Park

If you're not up for a long drive, then take a short trip from Manila Airport
and visit the most iconic place in the capital, Rizal Park. Also known as Luneta
Park, this staple in Manila tours covers approximately 140 acres of land surrounding
the Rizal Monument, near some of the best hotels in Manila. It is a place built to pay
homage to the Philippine national hero, Jose Rizal.


Masungi Georeserve

Masungi Georeserve has a strict no-reservation-no-entry policy. This is an effort
to manage the amount of foot traffic that walks through the reserve. Once you've
booked and confirmed your preferred date, ready yourself to be treated to an abundance
of flora, fauna, wildlife, and glorious rock formations.


Mt. Pinatubo

From one of the biggest natural disasters in the 1990s to one of the most sought-
after tourist destinations in the Philippines, Mt. Pinatubo has become a definite must
-try adventure destination in Luzon and one of the best places to visit in Pampanga.


Bangui Windmills

Standing tall on the northwest tip of Luzon are the Bangui Windmills in Bangui,
Ilocos Norte. These 230-foot windmills line up in a 9-kilometer stretch along the
Bangui Bay facing the West Philippine Sea, and is one of the best Ilocos tourist spots.


Honda Bay,Puerto Princesa

Cool off in the clear blue waters of Puerto Princesa's Honda Bay. This Palawan
tourist spot has some of the most pristine beaches that are home to diverse marine life,
numerous picturesque islands, and a hearty seafood spread.


Kayangan Lake,Coron

Also found in the southern tip of Luzon is Kayangan Lake in Coron, Palawan,
one of the best summer destinations in Luzon. The emerald-like beauty of Coron's
waters is famous all around the world.


Big Lagoon,El Nido

Beaches in the Philippines are plentiful, and some of the most beautiful ones can
be found in El Nido, Palawan, making it a top choice for couples who want to have
their honeymoon in the Philippines.


Calle Crisologo,Vigan

Calle Crisologo in Vigan is a beautiful and historic street. Its cobblestone pavement,
Spanish colonial architecture, and old-world charm make it a must-visit destination.
Explore the street's unique shops, try local delicacies, and immerse yourself in the
rich cultural heritage of Vigan. Don't forget to take lots of photos to capture the beauty
of Calle Crisologo!
